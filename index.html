Get Started with MEDS - 5min ⏱️

Shockingly Simple

MEDS (Medical Event Data Standard) is the simplest possible standard for health AI, making it easy to use with diverse data sources and modelling needs.

Health AI Ecosystem

MEDS empowers a high-performance and flexible health AI ecosystem to streamline your research via parallelism and sparse data structures.

Frictionless Reproducibility

MEDS ensures that your models, tools, and pipelines are easily reproducible across data sites, research tasks, and computational environments.
